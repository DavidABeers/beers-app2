Inventory Manager User Guide
============================
1) Adding items to the list:
    To add an item to the list, click "New Item"

2) Removing Items from the list:
    To remove only the currently selected item from the list, click "Remove Item"
    To delete all items from the list, click "Clear Inventory"

3) Editing Item Properties:
    To access an item's properties, click the field you wish to edit, and enter a valid input

4) Saving Your List:
    To save a list, click "Save"

5) Loading a List:
    To load a list, click "Load"
